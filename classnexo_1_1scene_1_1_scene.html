Detailed Description

Represents a scene in the engine.

A Scene encapsulates a collection of entities, a unique identifier, a name, and active/rendered states. It manages its entities via an ECS Coordinator.

Responsibilities:

  • Adding and removing entities.
  • Managing active and rendered states for the scene and its entities.
  • Providing access to scene metadata (name, ID, UUID).
